FMCSA Designated Employer Representative (DER)

This training satisfies Designated Employer Representative (DER) requirements under DOT 49 CFR Part 382 for FMCSA-regulated motor carriers.

Training Content

The Designated Employer Representative (DER) is responsible for oversight of the company’s DOT drug and alcohol testing program.

This module covers:

  • Program oversight responsibilities
  • Testing procedures & documentation
  • Recordkeeping requirements
  • Regulatory enforcement standards
  • Compliance management under FMCSA regulations
